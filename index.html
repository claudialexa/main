The Apps

AfterStorm: The Smartest Preparedness ChatBot

By using the latest cutting-edge messaging technology, AfterStorm provides users with the information they most want and need during weather emergencies.

BPrep: With Small Business Owners in Mind

By centralizing communications in one easy to use app, BPrep streamlines the process of contacting staff during an emergency situation, allowing business owners to save time and resources.

Civen: Engaging Citizens, Enhancing Communities

Civen empowers citizens to take charge of their safety and their communities by allowing them to report hazardous conditions such as flooding, burst pipes and potholes to emergency responders in just a few seconds.
